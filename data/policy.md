# Travel Reimbursement Policy

## 1. Hotel Expenses
- Maximum hotel reimbursement is ₹5,000 per night.
- Original receipt is mandatory.

## 2. Food Expenses
- Maximum food reimbursement is ₹1,500 per day.
- Receipt is mandatory for expenses above ₹500.

## 3. Transportation
- Taxi and cab expenses are reimbursable up to ₹2,000 per trip.
- Flight tickets must be economy class.
- Boarding pass and ticket invoice are required.

## 4. Miscellaneous Expenses
- Internet charges up to ₹500 are allowed.
- Laundry charges up to ₹800 are allowed for trips longer than 3 days.

## 5. Approval Rules
- Claims up to ₹10,000 are auto-approved if policy compliant.
- Claims between ₹10,001 and ₹30,000 require Manager approval.
- Claims above ₹30,000 require Finance approval.

## 6. Missing Documents
The following claims must be sent for Manual Review:
- Missing receipt
- Missing boarding pass
- Unclear expense category
- Incomplete claim information

## 7. Duplicate Claims
Duplicate reimbursement claims are automatically rejected.
